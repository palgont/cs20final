About Us

NomNomNom is dedicated to make healthy eating easy and sustainable by offering customizable or preset grocery bundles based on a weekly or biweekly subscription. Catering to various dietary needs—including vegan, vegetarian, meat & vegetables, halal, kosher, and more—NomNomNom helps users enjoy nutritious, well-balanced meals while reducing food waste. By delivering thoughtfully curated ingredients in the right portions, we make meal planning effortless and ensure that nothing goes to waste.

Our headquarters is located in Boston, but we serve meal enthusiasts nationwide. With a team of top-tier nutrition and tech experts, we ensure a smooth and seamless experience in planning, tracking, and enjoying every bite.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does NomNomNom work?
NomNomNom lets you choose from a variety of weekly meals. Once you select your meals, we deliver all the pre-portioned ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions straight to your door.
Do I need to be a good cook to use NomNomNom?
Not at all! Our recipes are designed for all skill levels. Whether you're just starting out or already love cooking, our step-by-step guides make it easy and fun.
Can I customize my meals?
You can choose from a rotating menu of meals each week, including vegetarian, gluten-free, and low-carb options. While we don’t offer full ingredient swaps, there’s plenty of variety to suit your tastes.
Where do you source your ingredients?
We partner with trusted local suppliers to provide fresh, high-quality ingredients. Everything is portioned and packed to stay fresh until you're ready to cook.
What if I have dietary restrictions?
We offer meals that cater to common dietary needs, like vegetarian or gluten-free. You can filter options when choosing your meals each week.
Do I need to be home for delivery?
Not necessarily. Our ingredients are packed with insulated liners and ice packs to keep everything fresh for several hours after delivery.
